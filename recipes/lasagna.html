Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  1. Meat This super meaty lasagna has sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef.
  2. Tomato products You'll need a can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  3. Spices and seasonings This lasagna recipe is flavored with fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  4. Cheeses Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese make this lasagna extra decadent.
